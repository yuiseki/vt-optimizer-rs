# Do No Harm

Purpose: Identify potential harms and mitigation practices for responsible use.

## Potential harms

- Distribution of sensitive locations or facilities.
- Re-identification risks from detailed POI or attribute data.
- Use of outputs for surveillance or targeting.

## Mitigations

- Generalize or simplify data where appropriate.
- Remove or restrict sensitive attributes and layers.
- Apply zoom limits to reduce detail.
- Review outputs before public release.

## Responsible use

- Use this tool for lawful, ethical, and humanitarian purposes.
- Avoid publishing sensitive datasets without review.
